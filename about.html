Who I am

I currently reside in the vibrant city of Los Angeles, California. My journey has taken me across three diverse countries, providing me with a rich tapestry of cultural experiences. Throughout my professional trajectory, I've had the privilege of working in two distinct countries, each contributing to my evolving skill set. Now, I find myself in the phase of an empty nest, witnessing my daughter embark on her own life journey after successfully graduating. My professional narrative has been equally dynamic. Commencing as an art director in advertising, I later ventured into the realms of graphic design and installation art. As my creative voyage unfolded, I seamlessly transitioned into the dynamic field of UI/UX design, where I've honed my skills for over 20 years. Beyond the professional realm, my passions extend to the love of travel, exploring new horizons and immersing myself in diverse cultures. Quality time with cherished friends holds a special place in my heart, and there's a unique joy in creating delightful home-cooked meals that bring people together.
