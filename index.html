Recipe Responsive App

Choosing the topic of recipes for this project stirs my appetite. Despite the multitude of recipe apps and websites saturating the internet, the question lingered: Why delve into a domain so extensively explored? The answer, however, proved remarkably simple.

Upon the arrival of my daughter and her introduction to solid foods, I encountered a unique challenge in nourishing her. She displayed reactions to various foods, making the feeding process quite daunting. To my surprise, the quest for websites accommodating ingredient substitutions proved futile.

In this culinary landscape, a significant gap emergedâ€”one that existing recipe apps had yet to bridge. Motivated by this unmet need, I saw an opportunity to contribute something distinctive to the culinary sphere, fueled by my person experience and the desire to offer a solution to others facing similar challenges.

My Project

Kain Tayo (Ka-in Ta-yo) translates to Let's Eat! A responsive web app, specializing on substitutions for ingredients with emphases on Filipino food.
